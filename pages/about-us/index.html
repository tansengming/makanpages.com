About Us

Where to eat ah?

MakanPages is here to help you make that decision.

Actually we had no clue either but we love the whole food blogging scene that has sprung up around KL. Isn't it so tribal that our friends or family members armed with cameras and good writing skills, go out there foraging through the various tastes and smells on offer to share their exploits with us (you too)?

Now we can have great food experiences and skip the stinky ones. Hurrah.

So how is it again that MakanPages is helping? Well we know that you don't want to read every single blog post out there on the internet. We know this because, hey man we too don't wanna read every single blog post out there. We just want to find the relevant ones, maybe see if our favorite food blogger has something to say about it, or if our friends or family like it.

MakanPages does this, we'll read through those bazillion blogs and kazillion posts. Ok , actually we built a fancy lil helper that does the reading that then generates a rating. The system isn't perfected yeMakanPages does this, we'll readore blogs and posts to improve upon our review ratings. We welcome your suggestions.

The rating quick guide

  • 1 - Terrible
  • 2 - Skip
  • 3 - Ok
  • 4 - Like
  • 5 - LOVE

MakanPages was launched in April 2011 by two guys that love the food in Malaysia. You can contact us at hello@makanpages.com

P/S - If you're a food establishment and want your place to be represented on MakanPages, please do contact us.

P/S/S - If you're a food blogger, reach out and say hi to us at hello@makanpages.com. We want to get to know you.
